Climb higher. Ride further.

So basically, Bicycle Hill Race is this physics game where you're constantly fighting uphill battles - literally. You control a rider pushing through steep climbs, tricky slopes, and unpredictable terrain while collecting coins and performing daring tricks. The farther you travel, the more opportunities you unlock to upgrade your ride and explore new routes.

The controls are pretty straightforward, which is nice because you can focus on not face-planting while trying to nail the timing on these crazy hills.

A classic hill climbing challenge.

Bicycle Hill Race brings back the classic style of physics-based driving games where momentum and balance determine success. Instead of traditional racing tracks, players must travel across rugged landscapes filled with steep hills, dips, and unexpected obstacles.

The physics actually feel pretty solid - your bike responds how you'd expect it to. Too much speed may flip your bike backward, while too little momentum can leave you stuck halfway up a hill. Learning how to control acceleration and timing becomes the key to progressing further.

Coins collected during the ride allow players to upgrade their vehicles, making it easier to tackle tougher terrain and extend each run.

Balance speed and control.

How it works

  • Accelerate to climb hills and maintain momentum

  • Balance the bike to avoid flipping over

  • Perform tricks during jumps to earn bonuses

  • Collect coins to upgrade your ride

  • Push further to reach longer distances

The terrain constantly changes, so every run feels different.

Choose the ride that fits your style.

Bicycle Hill Race features a variety of unlockable bikes designed for different driving approaches. Some bikes provide better speed on flat ground, while others handle steep climbs more efficiently.

Players can unlock more than ten different bikes as they progress through the game. Experimenting with different vehicles helps you discover which one performs best on specific terrain types.
